Revo Toys

Our journey began in 2016

Our company was born in October 2016, initially as a manufacturer of fidget spinners (this is before it became a global phenomenon!).

In March 2017, we launched and successfully raised a Kickstarter with the help of 167 backers to bring our spinners to market. This became a launch pad for Revo to bring many new products to market.

Since then, we've evolved and expanded our product line to include many other fidgets and novelties. These products all share a focus of being unique and trend-setting.

To date, our products are enjoyed in over 250 destinations in the world. This is made possible by our network of retail partners.

We have grand plans and we're just getting started.


Let's make this journey together.


If you need anything at all, don’t hesitate to reach out! We’re happy to help.

Thank you for shopping with us and learning about our story.
